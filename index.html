SYNOPSIS :

My Demon is the latest romantic fantasy Korean drama that brings together Song Kang and Kim Yoo-jung. In the play, Song Kang plays a 200-year-old demon. Meanwhile, Kim Yoo-jung becomes a successful CEO who is a workaholic. His drama tells the story of the feud between humans and demons that occurs in the modern world. A young woman has the ability to see and interact with demons. The woman is named Do Do Hee, who must one day get involved in a conflict between humans and demons who seek to ruin human life in the world.
Later in the conflict, Do Do Hee meets a demon named Jung Koo Won. Jung Koo Won falls in love with Do Do Hee and tries to live together. Of course, the love story between the two is not easy. They have to face the opposition between two different worlds.
With an interesting storyline and stunning performances from the cast, the drama MY DEMON is one of the dramas worth watching. In addition, the theme of the battle between good and evil is also able to captivate the audience, until Song Kang's latest drama is included in one of the popular dramas at the end of 2023.
